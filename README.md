# SpendZero

**No-spend challenge tracker • Build financial freedom one day at a time**

A beautiful, private SwiftUI habit tracker built with 100% on-device SwiftData. Track no-spend days, crush impulse buys, and watch your savings grow — all without your data ever leaving your phone.

## Features

- **Woofz-style onboarding flow** — 7-screen guided setup with spending quiz, category picker, challenge commitment, animated loading with social proof, and paywall
- **Daily no-spend tracker** — mark no-spend days, log spending by category, track impulse buys with coping strategies
- **Streak calendar** — month-view calendar with no-spend day indicators, savings timeline, and monthly summaries
- **Progress charts** — savings growth area chart, spending by category donut chart, impulse control ring chart with 7D/30D/90D ranges
- **Challenge library** — 10 pre-built challenges (7-Day Sprint, Coffee Detox, 30-Day No-Spend, and more) plus custom challenge creation
- **Impulse logger** — track urges to spend, log whether you resisted, record coping strategies, and see money saved
- **Daily win checklist** — track wins like making coffee at home, packing lunch, walking instead of Uber
- **PDF export** — generate shareable savings reports with stats, category breakdowns, and streak data
- **Home screen widgets** — small and medium savings glance widgets showing total saved and current streak
- **RevenueCat subscriptions** — paywall with weekly/monthly/annual plans and 3-day free trial highlighted
- **100% private** — all data stays on-device with SwiftData, no servers, no accounts, no tracking

## Tech Stack

- Swift 6 + SwiftUI
- SwiftData (100% local & private)
- RevenueCat
- WidgetKit + Swift Charts
- MVVM + @Observable
